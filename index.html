Welcome to the PIMV Evaluation Prototype

This demo shows how the Privacy Identity & Management Vault (PIMV) handles identity contexts, consent, GDPR erasure, and verifiable credentials.

Please explore the prototype, then complete the usability evaluation survey.

Research Prototype Notice

This PIMV system is an academic research prototype created for evaluation and usability testing. No real personal data is collected, stored, or transmitted. All data remains locally in your browser and is erased on refresh.

For questions or participation details, please contact the researcher. University ethics guidelines apply.
